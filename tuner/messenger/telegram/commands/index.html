§Telegram Slash Commands Controller

§Overview

Parses and handles Telegram slash commands (e.g. /model, /new, /abort, /diagnose, /cron). Validates permissions and updates active workspace states.

§Collaboration Graph

  • Called by process_text_with_files at early check cycles.
  • Instructs SessionManager to reset or resolve sessions.
  • Interacts with CronManager to toggle scheduled jobs.
